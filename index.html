Evolution Visualized
Welcome to our Human Evolution page! Here, young learners can explore how humans have grown, changed, and developed over millions of years. We created this site to help elementary students understand our past in a fun, clear, and exciting way. Get ready to discover amazing facts, meet our earliest ancestors, and learn how they helped shape the world we live in today!


We will start by introducing the main unifying ideas of biology, one of which being evolution. Then, we will discuss how the idea of evolution has changed over time until Charles Darwin's theory of evolution and natural selection. With visualizations, we will try to make this accessible.
